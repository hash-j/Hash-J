Ingredients

  • Ramen noodle block
  • Ramen flavor packet
  • Water

Optional Add-Instructions

  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Sriracha or hot sauce
  • Chopped green onions
  • Soft-boiled egg
  • Sliced cooked chicken or pork

Instructions

  1. Boil Water: Fill a pot with water and bring it to a boil.
  2. Cook Noodles: Add the ramen noodle block to the boiling water. Cook according to the package instructions, usually around 3-5 minutes.
  3. Add Flavor: Once the noodles are cooked, add the ramen flavor packet to the pot. Stir well to combine.
  4. Serve: Pour the ramen into a bowl.

Customize Your Ramen

  1. Add flavor: For a richer taste, add a splash of soy sauce or sesame oil.
  2. Spice it up: If you like it spicy, add a dash of Sriracha or hot sauce.
  3. Top it off: Garnish with chopped green onions for a fresh flavor. For a more filling meal, add a soft-boiled egg or slices of cooked chicken or pork.
