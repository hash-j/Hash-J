Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken or mutton (cut into pieces)
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 cup lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tablespoons red chili powder
  • 1 tablespoon cumin powder
  • 1 tablespoon coriander powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

Marinate the meat

In a large Bowl Combine:

  1. Yogurt
  2. Lemon juice
  3. Ginger-garlic paste
  4. Red chili powder
  5. Cumin powder
  6. Cumin powder
  7. Garam masala
  8. Black pepper
  9. Salt

Mix Well!

  • Add the chicken or mutton pieces to the marinade, ensuring they are evenly coated. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or preferably overnight.

Grill or Oven Cook

Grill

Preheat your grill to medium-high heat. Place the marinated meat on the grill and cook for about 20-25 minutes, or until cooked through, turning occasionally.

Oven

Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Place the marinated meat on a baking sheet and roast for about 40-45 minutes, or until cooked through.

Serving Suggestion

Sajji is traditionally served with naan or roti and a side of raita.

Tip

  • For the best flavor, it's ideal to marinate the meat overnight.
