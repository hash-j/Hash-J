Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Hamburger buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Cheese (optional)
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise

Instructions

Shape the patties

Take your ground beef and form it into round patties. Make sure they're a little thicker in the middle to prevent them from shrinking too much when you cook them.

Grill or pan-fry

You can cook your burgers on a grill or in a frying pan. Cook them to your desired level of doneness.

Toast the buns

While your burgers are cooking, toast your hamburger buns for a little extra crunch.

Make the Burger Suace

  1. Mayonnaise
  2. Ketchup
  3. Mustard (yellow or Dijon)
  4. Sweet pickle relish
  5. A pinch of sugar
  6. A little bit of vinegar (white or apple cider)

Instructions for making Sauce

  1. Mix it up: Put all the ingredients in a small bowl.
  2. Taste test: Try it and see if you like it. If it's too sweet, add a little more vinegar. If it's not sweet enough, add a bit more sugar
  3. Chill it: Pop it in the fridge for a little while to let the flavors blend.

Assembling

Place your cooked burger on the bottom bun. Add your favorite toppings like

  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Cheese
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise
Put the top bun on and enjoy!

Tip

  • For extra flavor, you can add seasonings like salt, pepper, or garlic powder to your ground beef before shaping the patties.
